## Compliance & Safety Guidelines

### Always Include

- ✅ Phone number (local or toll-free)
- ✅ "Free consultation" language
- ✅ Disclaimer if using case results or credentials
- ✅ Appropriate hashtags
- ✅ Call-to-action
- ✅ Proper office location reference if location-specific

### Never Include

- ❌ Client names (unless explicit consent)
- ❌ Unverified statistics
- ❌ Guaranteed outcomes ("You'll win this much")
- ❌ Comparison to other firms
- ❌ Legal advice (education, not advice)
- ❌ Inflammatory language about opposing parties
- ❌ Politics or controversial social issues

### Case Result Disclaimer Example

> "This is a historical case result. Your case may be worth more or less. Each case is unique. Results do not guarantee similar outcomes in other cases."
