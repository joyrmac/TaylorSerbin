## Content Pillars: 40-20-20-20 Model

### Pillar 1: The Rider's Life (40% of posts)

**Content themes:**
- Jason riding his motorcycle (action footage)

- Rider safety tips (from lived experience)

- Motorcycle culture moments (community gatherings, events)

**Example posts:**
- Saturday morning ride footage with safety reminder
- Local toy run participation photos
- Gear review based on actual use
- Riding route tips for the Carolinas
- Motorcycle club event coverage

### Pillar 2: The Gourmet Litigator (20% of posts)

- Behind-the-scenes kitchen footage
- Cooking technique demonstrations
- Local ingredient sourcing
- Entertaining at home (setup, prep, final dishes)
- Wine/food pairings
- Cooking equipment and kitchen reviews
- Food philosophy and approach

**Posting cadence:** 1 post/week (usually Thursday-Friday for weekend entertaining)

**Example posts:**
- "Making escargot tonight—here's how I do it"
- Kitchen prep footage with narration
- Final plated dish with cooking story
- Local farmers market haul
- Equipment review ("this cast iron is 30 years old")
- Entertaining tips for home dinner parties

### Pillar 3: Cigars & Five-Stand (20% of posts)
**Core:** Jason's hobbies in cigar collecting and clay shooting

**Content themes:**
- Cigar reviews and collecting stories
- Five-stand shooting competition footage
- Local shooting sports events
- Equipment reviews (shotguns, gear)
- Cigar lounge visits and recommendations
- Shooting technique tips
- Tournament results
- "Downtime" lifestyle moments

**Posting cadence:** 1 post/week (usually Sunday-Monday for weekend activities)

**Example posts:**
- New cigar acquisition and review
- Shooting event results with footage
- Cigar lounge ambiance and atmosphere
- Shotgun or gear review
- Tournament preparation
- "How I spend my weekends"

### Pillar 4: Alligator Wisdom (20% of posts)
**Core:** Legal tips and motorcycle safety education with The Litigator branding

**Content themes:**
- Motorcycle insurance tips and coverage guides
- Road safety information (driver awareness, hazards)
- Rider rights and legal information
- Personal injury law basics
- Common motorcycle accident scenarios (prevention focus)
- Legal misconceptions debunked
- "If you've been in an accident, here's what to do"
- Safety equipment importance

**Posting cadence:** 1 post/week (usually mid-week)

**Critical:** Every piece is educational/protective, not salesy. Goal is to help riders stay safe, not generate leads directly. But every piece subtly reinforces Jason's value: he cares about riders.

**Example posts:**
- "Check your mirrors before lane changes. 70% of accidents I've seen involve mirror blindness."
- "Insurance companies will lowball you. Here's what full coverage actually means."
- "Rider gear isn't optional. Physics doesn't negotiate with you."
- "Been hit? Document everything. Here's a 5-point checklist."
- "Your insurance policy has a deductible. Understand it before you need it."

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### The Tone: "Conversation Over a Cigar"

**What it sounds like:**
- Direct, not flowery
- Expert but not condescending
- Gritty, not polished
- Honest, sometimes blunt
- Accessible, not corporate
- Protective of riders/workers
- No bullshit

**Examples of ON-BRAND voice:**

✅ "Just rolled in from a 200-mile ride. Got some escargot going for tonight. If you're riding tomorrow, check your brakes first. Seriously. Insurance doesn't cover stupid."

✅ "Got asked to consult on a motorcycle accident case yesterday. Guy got lowballed $40K by insurance. Should've been $400K. Don't settle fast. Get a lawyer."

✅ "This cigar is overrated. Everyone says it's the best. It's not. Try the [alternative]. Save your money."

✅ "Shooting in the wind today was brutal. Taught me something—conditions matter. Same with evidence in the courtroom. Small details change everything."

**Examples of OFF-BRAND voice:**

❌ "Thrilled to announce my latest motorcycle adventure! #MotorcycleLife #Blessed #InfluencerMoment"

❌ "Motorcycle riding is a wonderful way to experience nature and connect with the community! @TaylorSerbin is honored to serve riders everywhere!"

❌ "As a legal professional with extensive trial experience, I would like to share some insights on the motorcycle accident litigation process..."

❌ "Wow! What an AMAZING day! Feeling so GRATEFUL for this beautiful LIFE! 🙏✨ #BlessedBeyondMeasure"

## Visual Style

### Photography Principles

- **High-quality but "raw"** — professional gear, authentic moments
- **In-the-moment** — not over-produced or staged
- **Action over posed** — riding, cooking, shooting (not smiling at camera)
- **Real backgrounds** — roads, kitchen, shooting range (not studio)
- **Natural lighting** — when possible, authentic lighting
- **Authentic colors** — not oversaturated or filtered to death

### The Alligator Logo

- Used to brand legal/wisdom content
- Distinct visual marker for "Alligator Wisdom" posts
- Connects Jason's persona to firm values
- Appears on education/safety posts

### Color Palette (For Graphics)

- Primary: Keep some Taylor|Serbin green for consistency
- Secondary: Black, white, natural motorcycle/food colors
- Accent: Alligator logo branding
- Overall vibe: Raw, not corporate-polished
