## The Decision: Which Approach?

### Approach A: Institutional Growth
**Best if:** Jason wants low personal visibility, Luana manages content, broad reach is priority
- ✅ Lower execution risk
- ✅ Compliant and corporate-safe
- ✅ Supports all practice areas
- ❌ Generic positioning
- ❌ Lower engagement
- ❌ Misses Jason's authenticity advantage

### Approach B: Jason Personal Brand (Luana's Recommendation)
**Best if:** Jason enjoys visibility, blue-collar positioning is priority, lead quality matters
- ✅ Authentic and differentiated
- ✅ Deep community connection
- ✅ Higher engagement
- ✅ Personal loyalty converts to clients
- ❌ Requires Jason involvement
- ❌ Requires consistent content capture
- ❌ More execution complexity

### Hybrid: Both in Parallel (Recommended)
**Best if:** You want reach AND authenticity
- Jason personal brand (Instagram, Facebook) = Engagement + authenticity
- Firm brand (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube) = Reach + credibility
- Both point to firm values
- Reuse content between them
- One strategy builds awareness, one builds loyalty
