## Problem Statement

**Current situation:** High organic visibility, poor conversion. Location pages have 0.14% CTR vs. blog content at 1.73% CTR.

**Root cause:** Location page strategy doesn't align with user intent or the firm's doctrine (clarity, restraint, authority).

**Goal:** Restructure website to improve conversion while maintaining SEO authority.

## Phase 5: Monitor & Optimize

**Timeline:** Ongoing (after launch)

### Metrics to Track
- [ ] Click-through rate (CTR) from search results
- [ ] Conversion rate (inquiries from landing page)
- [ ] Bounce rate (are users staying on page?)
- [ ] Time on page (are they reading?)
- [ ] Mobile vs. desktop performance

### Optimization Cycle
- Week 1-2: Monitor baseline metrics
- Week 3-4: Identify issues, propose small improvements
- Month 2: Implement improvements, measure impact
- Month 3: Major adjustments based on learnings
- Month 4+: Continuous optimization

### Success Criteria
- ✅ Location page CTR increases to 0.5%+ (from 0.14%)
- ✅ Inquiry conversion rate improves
- ✅ Page load time under 2 seconds
- ✅ Mobile bounce rate decreases
- ✅ User engagement (time on page) increases

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Reindex takes time | Delayed CTR improvement | Submit sitemap to GSC, monitor crawl stats |
| Content too long | Users don't read | Keep paragraphs short, use hierarchy |
| Design breaks mobile | Mobile users bounce | Test on multiple devices before launch |
| No clear CTA | Users don't know what to do | Add prominent, clear call-to-action |
| Page too slow | High bounce rate | Optimize images, reduce scripts, use CDN |
