## Approval Types

### Standard Posts (Luana Approval Only)

- **Social media posts** featuring pages, blog content, practice areas
- **Educational content** posts and tips
- **Team spotlights** (non-major announcements)
- **Generic firm messaging** (office hours, service area info)
- **Routine updates** and promotional posts

**Approval Process:**
1. Content creator drafts post
2. Submit to Luana for review
3. Luana approves or requests revisions
4. Post scheduled/published

### Critical Posts (Luana + Jason Approval)

- **Case results** featuring specific settlement/verdict amounts
- **Testimonials** with identifying details
- **Major announcements** (new hires, awards, major verdicts)
- **Specialist credential claims** or significant certifications
- **Sensitive case discussions** or public legal commentary
- **Any post with legal/compliance implications**

**Approval Process:**
1. Content creator drafts post
2. Submit to Luana with "ESCALATE TO JASON" flag
3. Luana reviews and routes to Jason
4. Jason approves or requests revisions
5. Luana confirms final approval
6. Post scheduled/published
