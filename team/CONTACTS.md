## Escalation Paths

### For Tone/Voice Issues
**Question:** Does this sound like Jason?  
**Escalate to:** Content Reviewer  
**Timeline:** 24 hours  
**Approval needed?** Yes

### For Legal/Accuracy Issues
**Question:** Is this legally sound? Factually accurate?  
**Escalate to:** Firm Contact  
**Timeline:** 24 hours (or ASAP for urgent)  
**Approval needed?** Yes

### For Strategic Questions
**Question:** Does this serve the overall strategy?  
**Escalate to:** Team Lead (review OPERATING_MANUAL.md first)  
**Timeline:** 24-48 hours  
**Approval needed?** Yes

### For Doctrine Violations
**Question:** Does this violate the Operating Manual?  
**Escalate to:** Immediately flag to team  
**Timeline:** Do not publish  
**Approval needed?** Yes, from lead + referenced doctrine section

### For Broken Process
**Question:** Is something in the workflow broken?  
**Escalate to:** Team sync meeting  
**Timeline:** Propose fix within 1 week  
**Approval needed?** Yes, via documented decision

## Communication Norms

### Daily Check-ins
- Format: Quick Slack message or email
- Content: Blockers, questions, status updates
- Frequency: As needed

### Weekly Sync
- Duration: 15 minutes (async okay)
- Content: Content submitted, reviews approved, metrics summary
- Format: Slack thread or email summary

### Monthly Review
- Duration: 1 hour
- Content: Performance analysis, strategy adjustments, team feedback
- Format: Video call or in-person

### Quarterly Deep Dive
- Duration: 2 hours
- Content: Full strategy review, metric analysis, roadmap adjustments
- Format: In-person or video call
