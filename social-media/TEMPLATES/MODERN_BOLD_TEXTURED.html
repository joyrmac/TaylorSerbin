Design System Reference

Colors

Primary Green: #006838 (backgrounds, overlays)
Dark Green: #01321D (text, depth)
Yellow CTA: #F6C01A (buttons, CTAs)
White: Text on dark, clean backgrounds

Typography

Headlines: Nunito Sans 900 (bold, aggressive)
Subheads: Nunito Sans 700
Body: Nunito Sans 400-500
All-caps labels: Letterspaced 0.15em+

Texture & Pattern

Alligator: Subtle dot/circle pattern (2% opacity)
Metal: Horizontal lines (polished look)
Grit: Grid pattern (rough look)
Diagonal: 45° lines for CTA posts

Modern + Bold + Textured

Clean layouts with generous white space. Aggressive typography (900-weight headlines). Textured backgrounds for depth. Bold color blocking (solid greens, yellows). Professional edge (no corporate polish).

Design Philosophy

Modern (clean, organized) + Bold (large, aggressive typography) + Textured (visual depth, patterns). Sophisticated but with grit. Professional but with edge. Minimalist structure with maximum impact.

Key Rules

• One accent color per post (green OR yellow)
• White space = breathing room
• Text always readable (4.5:1 contrast minimum)
• Alligator watermark optional (8-15% opacity)
• Texture adds depth, never overwhelms
