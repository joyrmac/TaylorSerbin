## Visual Identity Guidelines for REAL Posts

### Alligator Logo Usage

**Size:** 20-40% opacity, depending on context  
**Color:** Green (#006838) or Yellow (#F6C01A)  
**Placement:** Top corner, bottom corner, or watermark across image  
**Purpose:** Marks this as "The Litigator" authority — aggressive, protective  
**Never:** Hide it. Make it visible. It's the brand.

### Color Palette (No Deviations)

- **Primary Green:** #006838 (overlays, accents, bars)
- **Dark Green:** #01321D (text, depth)
- **Yellow CTA:** #F6C01A (phone numbers, urgent info)
- **Black:** #000000 (readable text)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (text on dark, backgrounds)
- **NO BLUE.** (Workhorse used blue. Stop.)

### Photography Standards

✅ **Real** — Actual motorcycle, actual people, actual office  
✅ **Gritty** — Raw lighting, real shadows, imperfect composition  
✅ **Purposeful** — Every image serves the message  
✅ **No AI** — No generated faces, no fake scenarios  
✅ **Authentic** — This is what it actually looks like  

❌ **NOT:** Stock photos, AI generation, over-filtered Instagram aesthetic  
❌ **NOT:** Sterile corporate office vibes  
❌ **NOT:** Fake scenarios or posed moments  

### Text Style (Overlays)

- **Bold, aggressive headlines** — All caps for impact
- **Direct messaging** — No legal jargon, no corporate speak
- **Protective tone** — "I'm warning you" or "I'm helping you"
- **Short sentences** — Mobile-readable, quick impact
- **White text on dark** — Always readable, always bold
- **Yellow for CTAs** — Phone numbers, urgent calls-to-action

## Caption Style (Text Below Image)

**Voice:** Jason's direct, honest, protective tone
