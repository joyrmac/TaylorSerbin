### Type 2A: Gourmet Litigator — Option 1
**Headline:** ESCARGOT  
**Subheadline:** This is how I do it  
**Caption:**
> Escargot with herb butter and white wine reduction. This is something I've perfected over 30+ years. Here's how you do it right:

**INGREDIENTS:**
- 24 escargot (or 2 cans frozen)
- 1 stick butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- White wine (1/2 cup)
- Sea salt and cracked pepper

**METHOD:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Melt butter with garlic and parsley
3. Place escargot in shells or ramekins
4. Fill each with herb butter mixture
5. Bake 12-15 minutes until golden
6. Serve with crusty bread and wine

## SECTION 10: PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDANCE

### For Instagram Captions
**Keep it:** 2-4 sentences, conversational, Jason's voice, direct  
**Example formula:**
- Real moment or fact
- Context or emotion
- What it means for clients
- CTA (subtle)

### For Facebook Posts
**Keep it:** Slightly longer (4-6 sentences), more explanation, accessible to older audience  
**Example formula:**
- Hook (question or statement)
- Explanation (2-3 sentences)
- Example or story
- CTA

### For LinkedIn (Firm Brand)
**Keep it:** Professional but authentic, 3-5 sentences, credibility-focused  
**Example formula:**
- Professional statement
- Why it matters
- Our approach
- Link to firm/values
