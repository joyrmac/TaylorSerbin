Gritty Design Rules

Photography (The Foundation)

  • Professional quality: Good camera, good light. But NOT polished.
  • Real backgrounds: Actual roads, actual kitchens, actual ranges. Not studio setup.
  • Film grain visible: Slight noise/grain visible in image. Shows authenticity, not over-processed.
  • Action > Pose: Jason riding, Jason cooking, Jason shooting. Not standing for camera.
  • Raw lighting: Harsh shadows OK. Real office lighting OK. Imperfect is better than polished.
  • In-the-moment capture: Not styled. Not manufactured. Not retouched to death.

Overlays & Texture

  • Film grain effect: Every template has subtle horizontal grain overlay (80-120% opacity on canvas)
  • Dark aggressive overlays: 70-90% opacity for photo-based posts. Make it DARK so text pops.
  • Gradient overlays: Sharp transitions (black to green, not smooth fades). Feels more dangerous.
  • Top accent bar: 6px solid green bar at top of every post. Non-negotiable brand mark.
  • Visible texture: Grain is VISIBLE, not hidden. Shows rawness, not polish.

Color Palette (Dark & Sharp)

  • Sharp Green #006838 — Accent bar, highlights, borders. Used sharply, not softly.
  • Dark Green #01321D — Overlays, backgrounds. The base of grit.
  • Black #000000 — Deep overlays, hard contrast, edge. Used aggressively.
  • White #FFFFFF — Text on dark ONLY. Must have hard contrast.
  • ZERO bright yellow. Not Jason's aesthetic. Save any yellow for only emergency CTAs.

Typography (Aggressive)

  • Headlines: Nunito Sans 900 weight. 28-36pt minimum. ALL CAPS. Letter-spacing -1px (tight). White on dark.
  • Body text: Nunito Sans 400 weight. 13-15pt. White on dark overlay. Line height 1.5+.
  • Minimal text on image: Max 2 short lines. Let image breathe. Captions carry heavy lifting.
  • No rounded corners on text. Sharp edges. Aggressive. Street-level, not friendly.

Alligator Logo Usage

  • Placement: Top right corner (25% opacity) OR large watermark background (8-10% opacity)
  • Size: Large enough to be distinctive. Not tiny. Not hidden.
  • Opacity: 8-25% depending on context. Visible but not dominating.
  • Every post gets it. It's the brand. It's the mark. Make it consistent.
  • Sharp edges: No soft halos. Clean, direct logo placement.

Design Principles (The Edge)

  • ✅ Texture visible: Film grain, rough edges, imperfections. That's the grit.
  • ✅ High contrast: Light text on very dark overlay. No muddy middle ground.
  • ✅ Sharp transitions: Color blocks instead of soft gradients. Black to green, not fade to fade.
  • ✅ Aggressive typography: Bold, caps, tight letter-spacing. Not friendly. Street-level.
  • ✅ Real imperfections: Grain visible. Shadows harsh. Lighting uneven. Shows authenticity.
  • ✅ Alligator featured: Not hidden. This is Jason's brand. Make it VISIBLE.
  • ✅ No polish: This isn't Instagram. This is a motorcycle bar. Raw and real.
  • ❌ NOT: Smooth gradients, soft edges, Instagram filters, rounded corners, "friendly" fonts, bright colors

Caption Strategy (The Story)

  • Image sets mood. Caption tells truth. Work together, but caption carries weight.
  • Jason's voice: Direct, gritty, honest. "Just rolled in from 200 miles" not "Completed bicycle excursion"
  • 2-4 sentences max. Mobile scroll is fast. Hit hard and move on.
  • Connect to values: Rider safety → "I've seen accidents." Cooking → "This is how I do it." Shooting → "Same discipline as trial."
  • Street-level energy: Bar-conversation tone. Peer advice, not lecturing.

What Makes This "Gritty Edge"

  • Visible texture: Film grain overlay creates rawness, not smoothness
  • Dark overlays: 70-90% opacity makes photos feel serious, dangerous, intense
  • Sharp color blocking: Transitions are hard (black → green), not soft fades
  • Aggressive typography: All caps, tight letters, bold weight. Not friendly.
  • Alligator featured: 8-25% watermark visible. This is Jason's brand, not corporate.
  • Top green bar: 6px border at top. Non-negotiable. Consistent. Sharp.
  • Real imperfection: Harsh lighting, visible grain, uneven edges. Shows authenticity.
  • Street aesthetic: Motorcycle bar, not Instagram. Raw, not polished. Real, not manufactured.

Image Sizes

  • Instagram Feed: 1080x1350 (vertical)
  • Instagram Story/Reel: 1080x1920 (full vertical)
  • Facebook: 1200x1500 (vertical) or 1200x628 (horizontal)
  • All formats: Top green bar 6px, consistent overlay, alligator logo consistent placement
🐊 The Core: This isn't a design system for Instagram perfectionists. This is for a trial lawyer who rides motorcycles, cooks escargot, shoots clay, and fights for his clients. Raw. Gritty. Real.
