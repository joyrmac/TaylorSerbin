Complete social media template system. Six content types × 2-3 design variations each. Lifestyle, educational, testimonials, case results, practice areas, attorney spotlights. Ready for Canva or Adobe production.

Template System Overview

Design Approaches (Applied Across All Types)

Approach A: Kompace Style
Bold organic color blocks (green #006838 + dark backgrounds). Geometric shapes (circles, lines). Clean photo integration. Structured-but-flowing. Jason's lifestyle focused.
Approach B: High-Contrast Bold
Solid color blocking (dark + bright accents). High-impact typography. Repeating geometric elements. Badges/circles for emphasis. Very direct, aggressive feel.
Approach C: Minimal Modern
Clean layouts. Generous white space. Green accents strategic. Simple geometric shapes. Approachable but professional. Works for legal/firm content.

Content Types (6 Major Categories)

• Lifestyle/Personal: Rider's Life, Gourmet, Hobbies (Jason's personal brand)

• Educational/Carousel: How-to guides, tips, lists, multi-slide information

• Announcements: News, updates, events, new services

• Testimonials: Client reviews, social proof, success stories

• Case Results: Settlement amounts, verdicts, case outcomes

• Practice Areas: Motorcycle accidents, workers comp, car accidents, wrongful death

• Attorney Spotlights: Jason, Larry, team introduction

Implementation Guidelines

Choosing Templates by Platform

Instagram Feed Posts: All approaches work. Approach A (Kompace) most authentic for Jason brand. Approach B (Bold) most impactful for firm brand. Approach C (Minimal) best for education/legal content.

Instagram Stories/Reels: Approach B works best (high contrast, visible at small size). Scale typography accordingly.

Facebook Posts: All approaches work. Approach C (Minimal Modern) performs best in News Feed. Include caption below image.

LinkedIn: Approach C preferred (professional, clean). Approach A works for Jason's personal brand.

Content Mix Recommendation

Weekly posting schedule (5-7 posts/week):

• 2-3 Lifestyle/Personal (Approach A)

• 1-2 Educational (Approach B)

• 1 Testimonial or Case Result (Approach B or C)

• 1 Practice Area or Announcement (Approach C)

Design Production Notes

All templates designed for:

• Instagram: 1080x1350px (feed), 1080x1920px (Stories/Reels)

• Facebook: 1200x1500px (vertical) or 1200x628px (horizontal)

• LinkedIn: 1200x627px (horizontal)

Build in: Canva, Adobe XD, or Figma. Export at 72 DPI for web.

Fonts: Nunito Sans family only (400, 700, 900 weights)

Colors: #006838, #01321D, #000000, #FFFFFF only (no exceptions)
