✅ Implementation Checklist

  • Size: 1080x1920 pixels for Instagram Stories (will convert to 3:4 or 1:1 later)
  • Typography: Clean sans-serif. Bold weights for headlines, regular for supporting text
  • Colors: Use the green palette provided. Avoid red entirely—swap for green
  • Images: Photo placement noted in each template. Quality images are key
  • White space: Keep breathing room. Don't overcrowd
  • Mobile-first: Test on phone. Text should be readable from 6 inches away
