Color System

Primary Green

#006838
Accents, borders, left bars. Used sparingly (2-3% of design).

Dark Green

#01321D
Text, depth, backgrounds. Headlines and serious content.

Yellow CTA

#F6C01A
Call-to-actions ONLY. Phone numbers, buttons, critical info. Stops the eye.

White

#FFFFFF
Backgrounds, breathing room, negative space.

Black

#000000
Body text, depth. All readable text should be black on white or white on dark.

Gray Text

#666666
Secondary information, less important text, metadata.

System Specifications

Typography

Display: Nunito Sans, Weight 900
Accent: Nunito Sans, Weight 700, Letterspaced
Body: Nunito Sans, Weight 400
Headlines always bold/extra bold. Never use decorative scripts. Letterspacing on all-caps labels (0.1em minimum).

Photography

Firm Brand: Professional, relevant to practice area. Motorcycles, construction, workplace, vehicles. Use Unsplash.
Jason Brand: Authentic, candid moments. Real life, not staged. Action photos > posed shots.

Layout Principles

Mobile First: All text must be readable on 4-inch phone screen without zoom.
Contrast: 4.5:1 text-to-background minimum for readability.
White Space: Generous margins. Never edge-to-edge collage.

Accent Usage

Green: 2-3% of post (left bar, accent line, borders).
Yellow: CTA only (phone, buttons, "click here", critical info).
One accent per post: Choose either green or yellow, not both unless separated.

Unsplash Suggestions

Motorcycles: "motorcycle", "bike ride", "highway", "crash", "helmet"
Workplace: "construction", "workers", "injury", "industrial", "warehouse"
Food: "food plating", "gourmet", "cooking", "restaurant", "cigar"

Text Overlay Tips

Bold Headline: 28-48pt, all-caps or title case, high contrast.
Dark Overlays: Use gradient (transparent top, opaque bottom) for readability.
Watermarks: Alligator logo 10-15% opacity, bottom-right.
CTAs: Always yellow background, dark text, 11pt bold, uppercase.
