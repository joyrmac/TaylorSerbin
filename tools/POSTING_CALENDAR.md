## How to Use This Calendar

### Before Posting
1. Check this calendar for available slots
2. Decide: Pillar? Platform? Which assets?
3. Add entry with: Date, Pillar, Platform, Caption (draft), Status: DRAFT

### During Review
4. Submit for approval
5. Update Status: PENDING_REVIEW
6. Reviewer approves or requests changes
7. Status: APPROVED or NEEDS_REVISION

### After Publishing
8. Update Status: PUBLISHED
9. Log engagement: Engagement Rate, Saves, Shares, Comments
10. Note any insights

### Monthly
11. Calculate metrics: Which pillar performed best? Which platform?
12. Plan adjustments for next month

## Cadence Tracking

### Instagram (4–5 posts/week)
- Target: Wed-Sat for optimal reach
- Mix: 40% Rider's / 20% Gourmet / 20% Cigars / 20% Wisdom
- Stories: 3-5 per week (real-time)
- Reels: 1 per week minimum

### LinkedIn (2–3 posts/week)
- Target: Tue-Thu mornings
- Mix: 50% Wisdom / 30% Rider's / 20% Balance
- Articles: 1 per month

### Facebook (3–4 posts/week)
- Target: Evenings/Weekends
- Mix: Mirror Instagram + community announcements
- Can be longer-form than Instagram

## Seasonal Adjustments

### Spring/Summer (March-August)
- **Increase:** Rider's Life (riding season)
- **Maintain:** Other pillars
- **Events:** Toy runs, rallies, seasonal rides

### Fall (September-November)
- **Increase:** Cooking content (harvest season, entertaining)
- **Maintain:** Rider's Life (fall riding)
- **Events:** Fall competitions, community events

### Winter (December-February)
- **Increase:** Cigars & Five-Stand (indoor hobbies)
- **Maintain:** Cooking (holiday entertaining)
- **Reduce:** Rider's Life (weather-dependent)
- **Events:** Holiday celebrations, New Year initiatives

## Content Buffer Management

**Target:** 2-week buffer minimum (8-10 scheduled posts)

- **Ideal state:** Posts scheduled for next 2 weeks
- **Warning:** Less than 1 week ahead
- **Critical:** Day-of posting (no buffer)

**How to maintain buffer:**
1. Collect content from Jason weekly
2. Plan content immediately upon receipt
3. Write captions within 24 hours
4. Get approval within 48 hours
5. Schedule 1-2 weeks in advance
