## Image Requirements

### Photo Specifications
- **Minimum resolution:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (vertical)
- **Format:** JPEG or PNG
- **File size:** Under 8MB
- **Quality:** High quality but "real" feeling (no heavy filters)

### Carousel Specifications
- **Number of slides:** 2-5 typically
- **Each slide:** 1080x1350px (vertical)
- **Consistent layout:** Same aspect ratio for all slides

### Reel Specifications
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (vertical video)
- **Duration:** 15-60 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Text overlays:** Recommended for accessibility
- **Audio:** Optional (captions help)

## Hashtag Strategy

**Instagram hashtag rules:**
- 3-5 hashtags maximum (no spam)
- Mix broad + niche hashtags
- Never use: #amazing #instagood #photooftheday
- Research trending hashtags in your niche

**Example hashtag mix:**
- 1 broad: #motorcycles
- 1-2 niche: #NCRiders #CooperativeRiding
- 1 community: #MotorcycleCommunity
- Optional personal: #JasonTaylor

## Story Strategy

**Use Stories for:**
- Real-time, behind-the-scenes moments
- Ephemeral content (less pressure to be perfect)
- Daily life updates
- Highlighting important posts

**Frequency:** 3-5 Stories per week minimum

**Format:**
- Phone video is fine
- Keep under 15 seconds per story
- Add stickers or text if it helps context
- Don't over-produce—authentic is better

---

## Reel Strategy

**Reel ideas:**
- Cooking process (30-45 seconds)
- Motorcycle moment (short clip + text)
- Cigar unboxing or review
- Legal tip explained (text overlay)
- Community event highlight

**Format:**
- Vertical video (9:16)
- Under 60 seconds
- Text overlays recommended (algorithm + accessibility)
- Music or natural audio
- Engaging hook in first 3 seconds

**Post as:** Reel (not regular video) for algorithm boost

## Engagement Protocol

**After posting:**
1. Monitor comments for first 24 hours
2. Respond to all comments within 24 hours
3. Reply as Jason (authentic, not corporate)
4. Engage with others' posts (shout-outs if relevant)
5. Note any DM inquiries (may be potential clients)

**Engagement tone:**
- Friendly but professional
- Answer questions directly
- Don't oversell
- Keep conversations brief (encourage DM if detailed)
