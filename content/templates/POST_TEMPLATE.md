## BEFORE/AFTER REVIEW

### Pre-Publish Checklist
- [ ] Message is immediately clear
- [ ] Caption under 150 words (or justified longer)
- [ ] Tone matches brand (gritty/authentic for Jason)
- [ ] CTA is natural, not forced
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant (not spam)
- [ ] Visual supports the message
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Links work (if applicable)
- [ ] Authentic to Jason (not manufactured)

### Authority Check
- [ ] Doesn't sound promotional
- [ ] Doesn't over-explain
- [ ] Sounds confident, not defensive
- [ ] Feels like Jason, not a marketing agency
