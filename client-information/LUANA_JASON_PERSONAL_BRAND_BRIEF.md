## Strategic Overview

Jason's personal brand serves as a **specialized bridge** connecting the motorcycle community with the firm's broader mission. While Taylor | Serbin has intentionally broadened its positioning away from motorcycle-centric branding, Jason's PERSONAL brand now preserves and deepens the firm's relationship with the riding community.

**The logic:**
- Firm brand = broad, diverse, professional, institutional
- Jason's personal brand = authentic, motorcycle-community-focused, peer-to-peer, "the human behind the suit"
- Together = both reach and authenticity

## Operational Protocol

### Jason's Role: "The Raw Feed"
- Captures photos and videos in the moment
- Sends raw content to agency
- Lives his life; doesn't over-produce
- Provides direction or context as needed
- Available for occasional "on-site" capture with agency

### Agency's Role: "The Pain in the Ass Work"
- Editing and post-production
- Caption writing (maintaining voice)
- Recipe formatting (testing, photography, instructions)
- Scheduling and batching
- Compliance review
- Account management

### Collaboration Model

1. **Jason captures content** → sends to agency
2. **Agency produces/edits** → formats for platform
3. **Agency writes captions** → maintains voice/brand
4. **Jason approves** → sends feedback if needed
5. **Agency schedules** → publishes on calendar

**Low friction:** Jason doesn't do production work. Agency doesn't determine content direction.
