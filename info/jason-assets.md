## Raw Content Capture

### Weekly Content Flow
1. Jason captures photos/videos
2. Organized by date and pillar
3. Social manager sorts and plans
4. Assets used for content creation

### Metadata to Capture
Each asset should have:
- Date taken
- Location
- Pillar (Rider's Life, Gourmet, Cigars, Wisdom)
- Platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, or multi)
- Brief description
- Any relevant context

---

## Asset Guidelines

### Photo Quality
- High resolution (minimum 1080px for social)
- Natural lighting preferred
- In-the-moment feeling over staged
- Authentic moments, not manufactured

### Video Quality
- Clear audio (if needed)
- Stable footage (use tripod for cooking)
- Under 60 seconds for Reels
- 9:16 ratio preferred for Stories

### Metadata Format
```
Filename: YYYY-MM-DD_Pillar_Description.jpg
Example: 2026-05-15_Riders-Life_Blue-Ridge-Ride.jpg
```
